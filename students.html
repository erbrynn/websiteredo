Prospective students:
I will be recruiting PhD students to start in Fall 2020.
If you are a prospective PhD student interested in working with me, please apply to the UMSI PhD program.

If you are a current UM masters student or undergraduate student interested in working with me, please email me with your CV/resume and research interests. I am particularly interested in working with students who want to conduct research on one or more of the following topics:

1) life transitions and social technologies
2) transgender and/or LGBQ+ people and social technologies
3) social media content moderation and marginalized populations.
